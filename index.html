      About ProjectHelper

      Interns at Hammad Technologies – ProjectHelper

      ProjectHelper is a professional academic discovery platform developed by Interns at Hammad Technologies to help students explore project topics, theses, dissertations, seminar papers, and research ideas from Nigerian universities.

      We built this platform to make academic topic discovery easier, faster, and more organized. Students can search by university, faculty, department, region, and document type.

      Our goal is to support research, innovation, digital learning, and academic growth across Nigeria.

      Our Mission

      To simplify academic research discovery for students by providing a clean and searchable platform for exploring research topics and project ideas.

      Our Goal

      To build a trusted digital platform that promotes academic innovation, project inspiration, and access to organized research information across Nigerian universities.
